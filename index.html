Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • 1. Is there a posting fee?
  • There is no posting fee! You can post on our site completely free!
  • 2. How do I post a successful ad?
  • Provide an honest description of the car that you are selling.
  • Include as much of the specifications of the car as you can (i.e. the model, the year it was made, the mileage, etc.).
  • Do some research and check and see what other people have listed their vehicle at and get an idea of the price range that you want to list your car for.
  • 3. How can I ensure a safe transaction?
  • You ALWAYS want to meet in person to avoid any potential fraud, bring a friend or a third party to accompany you. To ensure the safest transaction, meet them at a parking area of a police station.
  • 4. What are some things to look for from a used car?

  • 5. How are transactions closed?
